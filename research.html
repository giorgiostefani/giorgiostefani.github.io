Research

My research lies at the interface of Calculus of Variations, Geometric Measure Theory, Partial Differential Equations, and Optimal Transport.

My recent work focuses on fractional and nonlocal function spaces, distributional approaches, geometric inequalities, transport equations, and variational problems.

Research interests

  • Calculus of Variations
  • Geometric Measure Theory
  • Fractional Sobolev spaces
  • Optimal Transport
  • Partial Differential Equations
  • Geometric inequalities
  • Nonlocal variational problems

Current projects

I am currently coordinating the project NewFrac, devoted to distributional approaches to fractional spaces, fractional geometric problems, and fractional partial differential equations.
